Recently, I needed to decrease the time of _stop the world_ stage during GC work. 
During work on the problem, I found out many interesting jvm flags. In order to save 
the information in one place, I created a table with them.  

<table style="text-align: center; width: 100%; border: 1px #2e3e4e;">

----

$$ \text{heap} = \text{young generation} + \text{old generation} $$
$$ \text{young generation} = \text{eden} + \text{survivor} * 2 $$
$$ \text{survivor ratio} = \frac{ \text{eden}}{\text{survivor} * 2} $$
